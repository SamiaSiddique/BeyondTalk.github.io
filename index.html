Innovation in Communication

BeyondTalk is a communication app which is developed to facilitate communication between its employees and customers.

BeyondTalk Features

Following are the features we are providing in our application to ease communication

Harassment Detection

It detects any kind of harassment carried out through text messages by any user. It will ensure that no one exploits this facility provided by an organization for the sole purpose of bridging the communication gap.

Behavioral Analysis

It conducts behavior analysis by making employees take the MBTI test. An accurate profile of the personality type of each employee will be available to the organization’s management to take important organizational decisions.

Communication

BeyondTalk is developed to facilitate communication between its employees and customers. Users can communicate with each other through text messages and share images. It also allows users to make audio and video call.

About Us

Our Mission

BeyondTalk is a communication app which is developed to facilitate communication between its employees and customers. It provides messaging ,audio and video calling facility to its users. It ensures that its messaging feature is not put to the wrong use by any of its users by carrying out harassment detection. BeyondTalk also assists an organization’s management to carry out tasks such as team formation etc. in an effective manner by assessing the personalities of employees through behavior analysis.
